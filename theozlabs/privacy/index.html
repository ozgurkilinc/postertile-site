Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TheOzLabs handles information in its Canva apps, including SerialFlow, PosterTile, BookletFlow, and SheetNest.

Information we collect

TheOzLabs apps do not require a separate TheOzLabs account. We do not sell personal information and do not use third-party advertising trackers in the apps.

App inputs, such as serial settings, layout choices, and user-selected design content, are used only to perform the action requested by the user. Unless a specific feature clearly says otherwise, this processing occurs within the app session and is not stored in an external TheOzLabs database.

Canva platform data

The apps operate inside Canva and may access design content only when needed for the selected feature and only through permissions granted by Canva. Canva separately processes information under Canva's own terms and privacy policy.

Support communications

If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, and diagnostic details. We use it to answer the request and improve the app.

Data retention

Support correspondence may be retained as reasonably necessary for customer service, security, legal compliance, and troubleshooting. You may request deletion of support correspondence where applicable.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards and minimize requested permissions. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children

The apps are not directed to children under 13. Canva Education users should use the apps under their institution's policies and Canva's requirements.

Changes

We may update this policy as the apps evolve. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.
