TheOzLabs Support

Support is available for SerialFlow, PosterTile, BookletFlow, SheetNest, and other Canva apps published by TheOzLabs.

Email
hello.theozlabs@gmail.com

Typical response time
Within 3 business days.

Before contacting support

  1. Confirm you are using the latest published version of the app.
  2. Try the action again in a fixed-size Canva design.
  3. Record the exact steps and input settings that produced the problem.
  4. Include the Canva design type, device, browser or app, and any visible error message.

SerialFlow troubleshooting

SerialFlow requires a valid number range. End must be greater than or equal to start, step must be at least 1, and large runs may need to be split into smaller batches. Bulk Create and Data Connector availability can vary by Canva plan and surface.

What to include in a report

Please send the app name, a short description of the expected result, what actually happened, screenshots where appropriate, and whether the issue can be reproduced in a new test design. Do not send passwords, payment details, identity documents, or confidential design content.
