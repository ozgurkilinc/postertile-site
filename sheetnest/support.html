SheetNest Support

SheetNest does not require a separate login. Most issues can be solved with the checks below.

Quick checks

  • Use PNG or JPEG files you own, or a Canva PNG export ZIP.
  • Keep each directly uploaded image below about 7 MB.
  • Confirm the final width, height, quantity, paper, orientation, margin, and spacing.
  • Export only pages named “SheetNest Print Sheet…”.
  • Choose PDF Print, keep scale at 100%, and disable Fit to page.
  • Measure one test sheet before printing the full batch.

Report a problem

Open a GitHub issue and include the expected result, what happened, paper settings, item sizes and quantities, and a screenshot without sensitive information.
