Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

SheetNest is a Canva app published by TheOzLabs. It does not require a separate SheetNest account.

Files and design content

Files selected in SheetNest are processed in the user’s browser. To create print sheets, the app uploads selected images and generated guide layers to the user’s private Canva media library and adds them to the open Canva design. TheOzLabs does not operate a separate server or database that stores these files.

Information collected

SheetNest does not independently collect names, passwords, payment details, advertising identifiers, or analytics events. Canva may process account, design, and usage information under Canva’s own policies.

Third parties

The app uses Canva platform services to upload assets and create design pages. No content is sold to third parties.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the SheetNest support page.
