BookletFlow Support

BookletFlow does not require a separate login. Most problems can be solved with the checks below.

Quick checks

  • Use a fixed-size Canva design, not a Canva Doc or Whiteboard.
  • Import at least four original PNG/JPEG page files or a Canva PNG export ZIP.
  • Do not include pages already named “BookletFlow Sheet…” when importing from a Canva design.
  • Wait until all generated print sides are added before navigating away.
  • Export only the generated BookletFlow pages as PDF Print.
  • Print at 100%, double-sided, and flip on the short edge.
  • Always test one physical copy before printing the full batch.

Report a problem

Include what you expected, what happened, the source page count, selected paper, margin and gutter values, how many print sides were created, and a screenshot without private information.
