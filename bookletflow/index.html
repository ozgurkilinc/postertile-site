How it works

1

Import pages

Choose the original Canva PNG export ZIP or select at least four PNG/JPEG page files.

2

Review the order

BookletFlow calculates the correct front and back page arrangement and adds blank pages when needed.

3

Create print sides

Generate the booklet sheets inside Canva, then export only those pages as PDF Print.

Designed for accurate home printing

Print at 100%, double-sided, and flip on the short edge. BookletFlow adds optional fold guides, labels, and trim borders so you can test one copy before printing the full batch.

Private by design

BookletFlow does not require a separate account, advertising tracker, or external content database. Imported pages are processed in the user’s browser and generated print sheets are uploaded to Canva so they can be added to the open design.
