Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

BookletFlow is a Canva app that arranges user-provided page images into booklet print sheets.

Information processed

BookletFlow may process page images selected by the user, document dimensions, paper settings, margins, gutter settings, and the generated booklet sheets needed to perform the requested action.

How processing works

Imported pages are processed in the user’s browser. Generated booklet sheets are uploaded to Canva so they can be added to the open Canva design. BookletFlow does not operate a separate user account system or external content database.

Data we do not intentionally collect

  • No passwords or payment information
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No sale of personal data
  • No separate analytics or behavioral tracking service

Canva

Use of Canva is governed by Canva’s own terms and privacy practices. BookletFlow only requests the Canva permissions required to import design content, upload generated assets, and add pages to the design.

Retention

BookletFlow does not intentionally retain imported page content on an external server. Generated assets remain subject to Canva’s storage and retention practices once uploaded to the user’s Canva account.

Children

BookletFlow is a general productivity tool and is not specifically directed to children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app’s functionality or legal requirements change. The latest version will remain available at this URL.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the BookletFlow support page.
