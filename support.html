PosterTile Support

PosterTile does not require a separate login. Most problems can be solved with the checks below.

Quick checks

  • Use a fixed-size Canva design, not a Canva Doc or Whiteboard.
  • Make sure the open Canva document matches the selected paper and orientation.
  • Use the original PNG or JPEG file, not a screenshot of the Canva editor.
  • Wait until page generation finishes before navigating away.
  • Review all generated pages before downloading as PDF Print.

Report a problem

Include what you expected, what happened, your paper, orientation, poster-size settings, how many pages were created, and a screenshot without private information.
