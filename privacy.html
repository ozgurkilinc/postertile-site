Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

TheOzLabs apps do not require a separate TheOzLabs account, do not sell personal information, and do not use third-party advertising trackers.

App processing

Inputs such as serial settings and user-selected design content are used only to perform the action requested by the user. Unless a feature clearly says otherwise, processing occurs within the app session and is not stored in an external TheOzLabs database.

Canva data

Apps access design content only when needed through permissions granted by Canva. Canva separately processes information under its own terms and privacy policy.

Support

If you contact support, we use the information you provide to answer your request, troubleshoot issues, and improve the app.
