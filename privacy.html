Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PosterTile handles information when you use the PosterTile Canva app.

Information PosterTile processes

PosterTile processes the image file you select, the print settings you choose, and technical information made available by Canva that is necessary to generate and add printable pages to your design.

How processing works

PosterTile performs poster tiling in your browser. Generated tile images are uploaded through Canva’s asset services so they can be inserted into your Canva design.

Data PosterTile does not collect

  • No separate PosterTile account credentials.
  • No PosterTile-operated database for storing uploaded poster images.
  • No external advertising tracker.
  • No external payment information in the current version.

Retention

PosterTile does not intentionally retain your selected poster image on a PosterTile-controlled server. Content placed in Canva is handled according to your Canva account and Canva’s applicable terms and privacy practices.

Security

PosterTile is designed to minimize data collection. No internet service is completely risk-free, and users should avoid uploading content they do not have permission to process.

Children

PosterTile is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not specifically directed to children. Users should use Canva and PosterTile only in accordance with applicable age requirements and organizational policies.

Changes

This policy may be updated when PosterTile’s features or data practices change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.

Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the PosterTile Support page.
